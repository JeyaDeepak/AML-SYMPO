Tech Titans

-Clash Of Wits And Words!

Step into the "Tech titans", where sharp minds and quick reflexes are put to the test. Teams of two will face off in a high-speed technical quiz, racing to answer challenging questions. Only the top-scoring teams will advance to the next level—a technical debate. Here, the real battle of wits begins as teams tackle thought-provoking topics chosen by the jury. The highest-scoring team from the quiz gets to pick their stance first, but victory depends on the strength of your arguments and the clarity of your presentation. Get ready to engage, debate, and dominate the "Tech titans"!

Round 1

Rapid-Fire Technical Quiz

Objective : Teams of two compete in a fast-paced quiz that tests their technical knowledge.
Time : 30 minutes to answer a series of questions.
Task : Answer as many questions as possible in a limited time. The questions will cover a range of technical topics, and speed is crucial.
Outcome : The top-scoring teams will advance to Round 2.

Round 2

Technical Debate

Objective : Engage in a debate on a technical topic provided by the jury.
Time : 10 minutes to present your arguments.
Task : The highest-scoring team from Round 1 gets to choose their side first. Teams must then present strong, well-reasoned arguments and defend their position.
Outcome : Scoring is based on the quality of arguments, clarity of presentation, and overall impact. The team with the best performance wins the "Tech Titans" title.
