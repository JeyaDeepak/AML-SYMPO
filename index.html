about us

The Department of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning at St. Joseph's College of Engineering, OMR, Chennai, was established in 2021 with a vision to revolutionize the field of AI and machine learning. Our department is dedicated to advancing the frontiers of knowledge and innovation in AI and machine learning through cutting-edge research, comprehensive education, and impactful community engagement. We aim to create a vibrant ecosystem that fosters creativity, innovation, and collaboration, enabling our students, faculty, and industry partners to thrive.



  • Our Vision: Our vision is to be a global leader in AI and machine learning, transforming pioneering research into practical solutions that enhance the quality of life and promote sustainable development. We aim to foster a collaborative and inclusive environment, encouraging diversity and creativity. We also strive to drive economic growth and development, benefiting society as a whole. Our vision is to create a future where AI and machine learning are harnessed to drive positive change, improve lives, and transform industries.
  • Our Mission: Our mission is to advance knowledge and innovation through cutting-edge research in AI and machine learning. This involves exploring new ideas, developing new technologies, and collaborating with industry partners to drive innovation. We also aim to provide comprehensive education and training to students and professionals, empowering them to succeed in a rapidly changing world. Furthermore, we strive to foster impactful community engagement and collaboration, driving technological progress and sustainable development. Our mission is built on three pillars: research, education, and community engagement.
  • Our Goal: Our goal is to equip individuals with the necessary skills and ethical grounding to address complex challenges across various industries. This includes developing expertise in AI and machine learning, as well as understanding the ethical implications of these technologies. We aim to drive technological progress and sustainable development worldwide, improving the quality of life for all. Additionally, we strive to develop innovative solutions that transform industries and societies, making a positive impact on the world. Our goal is to create a new generation of AI and machine learning professionals who are equipped to tackle the challenges of the 21st century.
