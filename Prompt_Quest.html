Prompt Quest

-Quest for the Extraordinary

Unleash your inner innovator in "Prompt Quest"! This exciting event starts with a visual challenge—recreate an image using your prompt-engineering abilities. Those who excel will advance to a second round, where the task is to design a full newspaper using AI tools. It’s a race against time and a battle of creativity. Think you’ve got what it takes? Enter "Prompt Quest" and show us your AI-driven creativity!

Round 1

Image Recreation Challenge

Objective : Test your memory and prompt-engineering skills by recreating a displayed image.
Time : Participants will be shown an image for 30 seconds and the time for recreating the image will be 5 minutes.
Task : After viewing the image, you must recreate it using prompting techniques. Your goal is to match the original image as closely as possible.
Outcome : The most accurate recreations will advance to Round 2. The judge’s decision will be final.

Round 2

AI Newspaper Creation

Objective : Use AI tools to generate a newspaper.
Task : Create a newspaper layout, filling it with AI-generated articles, headlines, and images. The focus is on creativity, layout, design and the overall quality of the newspaper.
Time : Participants will have 60 minutes to complete the task.
Outcome : The participant with the most compelling and well-designed newspaper wins the "Prompt Quest" challenge.The judge’s decision will be final.
